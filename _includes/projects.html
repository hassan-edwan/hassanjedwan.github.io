Weightlifting Analysis

I created an app that tracks the path of a barbell throughout the entire range of motion of a lift by uploading a video of their attempted lift from the side profile. When multiple videos are uploaded, it allows the user to compare the bar path between the videos to ensure form consistency between the lifts attempted. The app additionally allows the user to perform a basic visual analysis of the lift and its bar path.
